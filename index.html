Giant Steps

Giant Steps is my weekly streaming radio show, featuring jazz, with a particular focus on bop, post-bop, and the good parts of modern and fusion.

The show airs on aNONradio and tilderadio at 0000 UTC on Fridays. I am usually on SDF com in the anonradio room, and tilde.chat IRC in the #tilderadio channel at showtime if you want to chat. Playlists and archive download links are posted to the aNONradio site. Enjoy!
